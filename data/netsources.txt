Below is a list of the datasets that have been used in the experiments, we only upload the small datasets to the repository.  


Academia: Academia online social network https://networkrepository.com/soc-academia.php
Advogato: Advogato is a social community platform where users can explicitly express weighted trust relationships among themselves. The dataset contains a list of all of the user-to-user links. https://networkrepository.com/soc-advogato.php
Anybeat: Anybeat online social network  https://networkrepository.com/soc-anybeat.php
AstroPh: Collaboration network of Arxiv Astro Physics  https://networkrepository.com/ca-AstroPh.php
Brightkite: Brightkite is a location-based social networking service provider where users shared their locations by checking-in. The dataset contains all links among users.  https://networkrepository.com/soc-brightkite.php
Citeseer: 	This is the citation network extracted from the CiteSeer digital library. Nodes are publications and the directed edges denote citations.  https://networkrepository.com/citeseer.php
Convote: 
Dolphins:  A social network of bottlenose dolphins. The dataset contains a list of all of links, where a link represents frequent associations between dolphins.  https://networkrepository.com/soc-dolphins.php
Douban: Douban is an online social network providing user review and recommendation services for movies, books, and music. The dataset contains all links among users. https://networkrepository.com/soc-douban.php
EmailUniv: Email communication network at the University Rovira i Virgili in Tarragona in the south of Catalonia in Spain. Nodes are users and edges indicate that at least one email was sent.
Erdos992: Erdos collaboration network https://networkrepository.com/ca-Erdos992.php
GooglePlus: Google-plus online social network https://networkrepository.com/soc-google-plus.php
Gowalla: a location-based social networking https://networkrepository.com/soc_gowalla.php
Gplus: Google-plus online social network
GrQc: Collaboration network of Arxiv General Relativity https://networkrepository.com/ca-GrQc.php
Hamster: This Network contains friendships and familylinks between users of the website hamsterster.com. https://networkrepository.com/petster-hamster.php
HepPh: Collaboration network of Arxiv High Energy Physics https://networkrepository.com/ca-HepPh.php
Karate: Zachary karate club network https://networkrepository.com/soc-karate.php
MathSciNet: Collaboration Networks https://networkrepository.com/ca-MathSciNet.php
Netscience: Co-authorship of scientists in network theory & experiments https://networkrepository.com/ca-netscience.php
NipsEgo: A social friendship network extracted from Facebook consisting of people (nodes) with edges representing friendship ties. https://networkrepository.com/socfb-nips-ego.php
PagesCompany: 	Mutually liked facebook pages. Nodes represent the pages and edges are mutual likes among them. https://networkrepository.com/fb-pages-company.php
PagesGovernment: 	Mutually liked facebook pages. Nodes represent the pages and edges are mutual likes among them. https://networkrepository.com/fb-pages-government.php
Reed98 A social friendship network extracted from Facebook consisting of people (nodes) with edges representing friendship ties.  https://networkrepository.com/socfb-Reed98.php
Themarker TheMarker Cafe is an Israelis online social network site that allows its member to connect and interact. Since most of its members are Israeli, most interaction and communication among members is done in Hebrew.  https://networkrepository.com/soc-themarker.php
TwitterFollows Twitter follower network https://networkrepository.com/soc-twitter-follows.php
USFCA72 Facebook social network https://networkrepository.com/socfb-USFCA72.php
WikiTalk https://snap.stanford.edu/data/wiki-Talk.html
WikiVote https://snap.stanford.edu/data/wiki-Vote.html
